Mission Statement

We exist to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ, sharing His love through service, and transforming lives in our community and beyond. We are committed to teaching the Word of God, building a vibrant and welcoming community, and equipping believers to live out their faith in practical ways.

Church Purpose

Glorifying God: The primary aim of any church is to worship and honor God. Making Disciples: Spreading the message of Jesus and nurturing believers. Service and Outreach: Helping others through acts of love and charity. Community and Fellowship: Building strong, supportive relationships among members. Equipping Believers: Encouraging growth in faith and practical application in daily life..

Metro Jesus Evangelical Church

Community and Fellowship: Churches serve as a hub for community, offering a place where people can connect, support one another, and grow together in faith. Fellowship opportunities often include social gatherings, study groups, and ministry programs that address the needs of the congregation and the wider community. Ministry and Outreach: Churches are involved in local and global outreach, offering services such as charity work, food banks, counseling, and mission trips. They aim to serve and share the love of Christ with people in need..
